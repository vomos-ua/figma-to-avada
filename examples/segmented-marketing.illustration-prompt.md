# Segmented Marketing — Illustration

**Aspect ratio:** 1.7:1 wide landscape (~1750×1030 px) — fits Figma placeholder ~631×371
**Placement:** Right column of ЛОГІКА ВКЛЮЧЕНЬ І ВИКЛЮЧЕНЬ block (alongside Include + Exclude vertical stack)
**Theme:** Dark navy background (matches section bg #0D0D26)

ChatGPT-Image prompt:

```
Create a clean, modern flat UI illustration in WIDE LANDSCAPE format (aspect ratio ~1.7:1, ~1750x1030 pixels) for a B2B marketing landing page section called "Segmented Marketing". Dark navy theme. The image visualizes: ONE cold audience pool flows into THREE parallel behavioral segments, each receiving a custom ad creative, then all converging into a single conversion funnel.

STYLE:
- Flat-3D / isometric vector style
- Dark navy background (#0D0D26) with subtle blue glow accents in corners
- White cards (for content with photos) and translucent-dark cards (#10143A) with 16px rounded corners, very soft drop shadows
- Connecting arrows: short dotted/dashed lines with arrowheads, in pastel blue (#7BB8F5), pastel purple (#A8B5F0), pastel teal (#7DD3C7) — one color per segment
- Brand accent color: #2134EA used for badges, key icons, segment headers
- White text on dark cards, blue accent text on white cards
- Sparing use of color, dominated by white-on-dark contrast

COMPOSITION (left-to-right horizontal flow):

LEFT (1/5 of canvas):
- One white card titled "Cold Audience" with LP-blue badge
- Inside: grid of 9-12 small photorealistic miniature avatars (diverse business professionals)
- Small placeholder text below avatars suggesting filtering params

MIDDLE-LEFT (1/5 of canvas, vertical stack of 3 cards):
- THREE small white cards stacked vertically: SEGMENT 01, 02, 03 with thin blue top accent line
- Card 1 (top): "Cold" badge, mini avatar + Instagram-pink dot
- Card 2 (middle): "Warm" badge, mini avatar + 25-50% percentage
- Card 3 (bottom): "Hot" badge, mini avatar + flame icon
- Three short dotted arrows diverge from the Cold Audience card on left to each segment card (different pastel colors)

MIDDLE-RIGHT (1/5 of canvas, vertical stack of 3 ad creatives):
- THREE small white cards representing creatives:
- Card 1 (Cold): tiny video-thumbnail mockup with play button, label "Audience-based video"
- Card 2 (Warm): tiny laptop showing dashboard, label "Explainer video"
- Card 3 (Hot): tiny offer-page mockup with CTA, label "Direct offer + remarketing"
- Each ad card connects from its matching segment card with a short dotted arrow

RIGHT (2/5 of canvas, converging funnel):
- Browser/landing-page mockup at top: rounded corners, 3 traffic-light dots, mock URL bar, hero mountain image, 3 placeholder text lines, blue CTA button
- Below browser: single user profile circle (white card, blue user silhouette, checkmark badge)
- Below user: "Conversion" card with purple-to-violet gradient circle + white trophy icon, 2 placeholder lines next to it
- Three dotted arrows from the 3 ad creative cards converge into the browser mockup
- Down arrow from browser to user circle
- Down arrow from user circle to conversion card

FLOW SUMMARY (left to right):
audience pool → 3 segments → 3 creatives → 1 landing → 1 user → 1 conversion

Avoid: heavy gradients, bright competing colors, text-heavy labels, light background, photorealism for everything (only tiny content inside cards).

Tone: professional B2B SaaS, dark mode, clean, structured, slightly futuristic.
```
